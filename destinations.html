Plan your major destinations in FRANCE

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France, located in the north-central part of the country. It’s one of the most iconic cities in the world, known for its rich history, art, fashion, and architecture.

Marseille

Marseille is a port city in southern France on the Mediterranean coast. It’s the second-largest city in France and the largest port in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d’Azur region.

Lyon

Lyon is a major city in southeastern France, known for its rich history, gastronomy, and vibrant culture.
